01 — Standard Clean

Standard Residential Clean

Best for: Regular home maintenance, recurring clients, homes that are already generally tidy.

Kitchen — countertops, stovetop, appliance exteriors, microwave interior, sink, cabinet wipe-down, sweep & mop
Living room — dusting, baseboards, mirrors, vacuum or mop, light switches & handles
All bedrooms — dusting, baseboards, mirrors, vacuum or mop, windowsills
All bathrooms — toilet, sink, shower/tub, mirror, floors, baseboards
Trash emptied and relined in all rooms
Final walkthrough and before/after photos

Before and after photos sent immediately upon completion
